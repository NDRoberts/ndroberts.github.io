About Me

Once upon a time, I completed a Bachelor of Arts degree in German (minor in philosopy!) and stepped out into the wider world as a college graduate, ready to do... whatever it was I thought I was going to do with that degree. There is a chance I hadn't thought it through very carefully. The follies of youth, am I right? Instead of that, I spent the next eight years working in inpatient mental health. It wasn't what I was expecting, but it was an unprecedented opportunity to learn and grow. Plus, great stories to tell at parties. Feel free to ask me.

At some point, I found that I had reached the limits of my personal and professional development on that path, and resolved to make some big changes. I thought about the things I could do that were not only interesting to me, but that were in slightly greater demand than German. The answer was pretty obvious when I realized that I have consistenly had two great loves in my life - working with computers, and making things difficult for myself. Back to school I went.

It took me three years and just about all of my life savings, but I successfully completed a B.S. in Computer Science in December of 2020. Now, I'm ready to bring my unusual range of experience to the world of software development, along with a growth mindset, an insatiable curiosity, and a well-developed sense of what's important in life. I'm genuinely excited by the idea that, through both my personal and professional work, I have the chance to make the world a little better for everyone.

For now, let's just say email me at ndroberts@live.com
